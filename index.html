AboutMustafa Mahmoud

A deep dive into 19+ years of engineering leadership, design, and automation.

Bridging Design Intent with Technical Performance

I am a competent Architectural Engineer and BIM Manager offering a rich history of proven leadership in architectural working drawings, advanced Revit modeling, onsite construction investigations, and end-to-end BIM process implementation.

Throughout my career at leading firms such as Pace and the Kuwait Technical Consulting Bureau (KTCB), I have spearheaded multidisciplinary BIM coordination on high-profile developments, including Kuwait University Health Sciences Center structures and Kuwait Gulf Oil Company (KGOC) Headquarters.

My unique value lies in the intersection of architecture, structural engineering, and programming. By using Python and Dynamo, I develop custom Revit scripts and plugins that bypass out-of-the-box software limitations, cutting production drawing times by up to 80% while upholding rigorous quality control.

Revit Automation

Quality Control & Clash Detection
